Our Philosophy

We celebrate the rhythm of the seasons. Every menu is crafted from sustainably-sourced produce and ethically raised proteins, prepared with techniques that honor flavor clarity and texture.

Wine & Cellar

A rotating cellar of 400+ bottles. Our sommelier pairs wines by terroir and texture to complement each course — old world and thoughtful new world selections.

Private Dining

Two private rooms: The Observatory (up to 16 guests) and The Hearth (up to 8 guests). Custom tasting experiences available for celebrations and corporate events.

Chef & Team

Our kitchen is led by a team of internationally trained chefs and hospitality professionals.

ML

Marcel Laurent — Executive Chef

A protege of Parisian ateliers and a passion for fire-based technique. Marcel leads menu development and kitchen philosophy.

Specialties: Modern French, Charcoal-grilled proteins
AR

Aisha Rahman — Head Pastry Chef

Delicate patisserie and modern dessert composition. Aisha designs plated desserts and pastry service.

Specialties: Plated desserts, contemporary patisserie
SO

Santiago Ortega — Sous Chef

Execution and seasonal produce programs. Santiago manages the garde manger and coordinates with local farms.

Specialties: Vegetable-forward cuisine, fermentation
VR

Vera Rossi — Sommelier

Curates the cellar and designs course-by-course wine pairings to highlight minerality and texture.

Specialties: Old World Burgundy, Natural wines
RK

Rohan Kapoor — Head of Service

Leads floor operations, service training, and guest experience — blending warm hospitality with precision service.

LM

Ling Mei — Beverage Director
